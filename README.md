### ✦  ＷＨＹ ＴＨＩＳ ＥＸＩＳＴＳ
In high-end venues, speed and aesthetic consistency are paramount. Standard macros often look cluttered or require complex HUDs that eat up your screen space and viewer resources. 

**This file (`auto_replace_master.xml`) is a standard, natively supported text-replacement list used by your viewer.** 
By importing this configuration, you are simply loading a carefully curated dictionary of shortcuts that your viewer will automatically expand when you type them. It is lightweight, secure, and utilizes the viewer's built-in functionality exactly as intended by the developers.

### ⟡  ＨＯＷ ＴＯ ＩＮＳＴＡＬＬ （ＦＩＲＥＳＴＯＲＭ）

**1. The Download**
Save the `auto_replace_master.xml` file directly from this repository to your computer.

**2. The Import**
In Firestorm, navigate to **Preferences ➝ Chat ➝ Typing ➝ Auto-Replace**. Click **[ Import ]**, select the downloaded XML file, and choose **[ Replace ]**.

**3. The Activation**
Select your new **"Aura - Premium"** list and click the **[ UP ARROW ]** until it sits at the absolute top. Ensure **"Enable auto-replacement"** is checked.

*You are now equipped with the Aura Engine vocabulary.*
